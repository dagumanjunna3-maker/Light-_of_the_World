    History of Tangub City

    Local lore has come up with several versions on how the name "Tangub" came about,
    but all of these stories carry one common thread: the name originated from the word
    "tangkub," the local name for rice baskets made of rattan strips that the Subanens carried on their backs.
    One such version tells us of lost Spanish soldiers who chanced upon a Subanen couple.
    Asked what the name of the place was, the couple replied, "Tangkub", thinking they were
    asked what they were carrying. Tangkub" later became "Tangub" giving name to the frontier town in Panguil Bay.
