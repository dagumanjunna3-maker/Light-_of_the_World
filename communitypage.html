    Women’s Welfare and Empowerment Programs
    Tangub City actively promotes gender equality and women’s welfare through initiatives
    funded by its Gender and Development (GAD) fund. These programs include welfare support,
    youth involvement activities, nutrition provision for children and pregnant women, and educational
    campaigns on women’s rights conducted at the barangay level.
    Support for Women’s Associations and Livelihood
    The city government encourages women’s participation in community development by providing
    financial incentives to members of women’s associations during the annual National Women’s Month celebration.
    These associations, with thousands of registered members, also receive support such as training
    (e.g., making local products like piñato and malunggay polvoron)
    and equipment like sewing machines during the COVID-19 pandemic to help sustain livelihoods.

    Anti-Drug Abuse and Rehabilitation Services
    Tangub City implements comprehensive anti-drug abuse programs through its City Anti-Drug Abuse Office.
    This includes community-based drug rehabilitation programs, aftercare and reintegration services
    for graduates of rehabilitation, drug education and advocacy campaigns, and support to
    Barangay Anti-Drug Abuse Councils.

    Police-Led Community Outreach Activities
    The Tangub City Police Station regularly conducts community outreach programs that serve children and families.
    Activities such as the distribution of school bags, food, and interactive programs
    (like mobile library storytelling) at local schools foster positive police–community
    relations and support children’s education.

    Culture of Volunteerism and Civic Participation
    Beyond structured programs, Tangub City nurtures a strong culture of volunteerism.
    Events like the Christmas Symbols Festival, for example, are sustained by the collective efforts
    of volunteers from all walks of life, embodying a community spirit where citizens willingly
    contribute their time, skills, and resources toward shared goal.
